Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted cheese on top. Baked until golden and bubbly, it’s a hearty and flavorful comfort food enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 500g ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste (optional for richness)

Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles
  2. Make the Meat Sauce
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  4. Preheat the Oven
  5. Assemble the Lasagna
