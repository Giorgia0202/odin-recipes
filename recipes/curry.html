Curry

Curry is a flavorful dish made with a mix of spices, herbs, and often a sauce or gravy. It can include meat, vegetables, or legumes and is commonly served with rice or bread. Curries vary in taste and spice level depending on the region, such as Indian, Thai, or Malaysian styles.
